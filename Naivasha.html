Naivasha

Welcome To Naivasha

Nestled amid the breathtaking landscapes of Kenya, Naivasha offers a serene escape for travelers seeking tranquility and natural beauty. Surrounded by lush greenery and the stunning waters of Lake Naivasha, this enchanting destination boasts an array of adventures waiting to be discovered. From boat safaris amidst a rich diversity of birdlife to leisurely walks through the majestic gorges, Naivasha captivates visitors with its idyllic charm. Explore nearby wildlife sanctuaries, encounter grazing giraffes and majestic hippos, or simply unwind in the comfort of luxurious lodges overlooking the picturesque scenery. Whether you're a nature enthusiast or seeking a peaceful retreat, Naivasha promises an unforgettable experience in the heart of Kenya's wilderness.

Enashipai Resort and Spa

Enashipai is a Maasai word meaning “A Place of Happiness!” Located within the splendor of the Great Rift Valley and by the shores of Lake Naivasha, Enashipai Resort and Spa is a multi award-winning leisure and conference destination offering authentic African hospitality focused on wellness.

Enashipai Resort and Spa offers a serene escape nestled in the heart of Kenya's scenic Rift Valley. With its lush surroundings and tranquil ambiance, it's the perfect retreat for those seeking relaxation and rejuvenation. The resort boasts luxurious accommodations, world-class spa facilities, and a range of recreational activities, including swimming, nature walks, and cultural experiences. Whether you're looking to unwind by the poolside, indulge in a pampering spa treatment, or explore the natural beauty of the region, Enashipai Resort and Spa promises an unforgettable experience tailored to soothe the mind, body, and soul.

Chui Lodge

Chui Lodge, situated in the heart of the Oserengoni Wildlife Sanctuary, comprises of 8 luxurious cottages set in a beautifully manicured garden. Enjoy international standard cusine under the shade of our acacia trees whilst you watch the wildlife drinking from the nearby waterhole.

Chui Lodge offers an exclusive safari experience in the heart of Kenya's wilderness. Nestled within the iconic Rift Valley, this luxurious lodge provides discerning guests with unparalleled comfort and serenity. With its intimate atmosphere and stunning surroundings, Chui Lodge is the perfect retreat for those seeking an authentic African safari adventure. From elegant accommodations to gourmet dining and thrilling wildlife encounters, every aspect of your stay at Chui Lodge is tailored to create memories that last a lifetime. Experience the magic of Kenya's wildlife and landscapes at Chui Lodge.
