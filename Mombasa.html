Mombasa

Welcome To Mombasa

Mombasa, located along the picturesque coast of Kenya, is a vibrant city renowned for its rich history, stunning beaches, and cultural diversity. As one of East Africa's major port cities, Mombasa offers a unique blend of Swahili, Arabic, and European influences, creating a captivating atmosphere for travelers. Visitors can explore the city's historic landmarks such as Fort Jesus, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, which stands as a symbol of the region's past struggles and triumphs. Beyond its historical treasures, Mombasa boasts pristine white sand beaches lapped by the warm waters of the Indian Ocean, making it an ideal destination for sun-seekers and water sports enthusiasts.

Voyager Beach Resort

Voyager Beach Resort is a vibrant oceanfront getaway in Mombasa, Kenya, offering a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure. Nestled on the shores of the Indian Ocean, its Swahili-inspired architecture and lush gardens create a welcoming ambiance. The resort features spacious accommodations, diverse dining options, and a range of activities including water sports and entertainment.

From cozy rooms to family suites, guests can enjoy a relaxing stay and indulge in buffet-style meals with international and local cuisine. With amenities like poolside lounging, beach activities, cultural shows, and a kids' club, Voyager Beach Resort ensures a memorable vacation experience for travelers of all ages.

Bamburi Beach

Bamburi Beach is a stunning stretch of coastline located near Mombasa, Kenya. Known for its powdery white sands and crystal-clear waters, it offers visitors a picturesque tropical paradise. The beach is lined with palm trees, providing shady spots for relaxation and enjoying the breathtaking views of the Indian Ocean.

Bamburi Beach is also a popular destination for water sports enthusiasts, with opportunities for snorkeling, swimming, and diving in the vibrant marine ecosystem. With its serene atmosphere and natural beauty, Bamburi Beach is the perfect spot for sun-seekers and nature lovers alike.

Sarova Whitesands Beach

The Sarova Whitesands Beach Resort and Spa is a luxury hotel located on the idyllic shores of the Indian Ocean, on the North Coast of Mombasa, Kenya. It is nestled amongst towering palms and lush gardens and is known for its excellent service and cuisine. The resort boasts five swimming pools, including a quiet pool, a pool bar, and a waterslide.

The hotel also has a spa with outdoor treatment decks that offer stunning views of the ocean. The Sarova Whitesands is a popular destination for families, couples, and business travelers alike. It has been rated among the best hotels in Mombasa and has won awards for its luxury accommodations and beachfront location.
