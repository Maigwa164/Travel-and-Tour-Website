Maasai Mara

Welcome To Maasai Mara

The Maasai Mara, located in southwestern Kenya, is one of Africa's most iconic and spectacular safari destinations. This vast expanse of savannah teems with an abundance of wildlife, including the renowned Big Five (lion, elephant, buffalo, leopard, and rhinoceros), as well as countless other species such as cheetahs, zebras, giraffes, and wildebeests.What sets the Maasai Mara apart is the annual Great Migration, where millions of wildebeests, zebras, and other herbivores cross the Mara River in search of fresh grazing grounds, creating one of the most awe-inspiring natural spectacles on Earth. In addition to game drives, visitors to the Maasai Mara can also experience hot air balloon safaris offering panoramic views of the savannah at sunrise, guided nature walks with Maasai warriors, and cultural visits to Maasai villages to learn about their traditional way of life.

Mara Intrepids Camp Masai Mara

Mara Intrepids Camp is a renowned safari camp located in the heart of the Masai Mara National Reserve in Kenya. The camp offers spacious and comfortable tented accommodations, each featuring modern amenities and stunning views of the surrounding wilderness.

Guests can enjoy thrilling game drives in the reserve, where they have the chance to witness the Big Five and other iconic wildlife species in their natural habitat. The camp also offers guided nature walks, cultural visits to Maasai villages, and hot air balloon safaris, providing guests with a comprehensive safari experience.

Saruni Eagle View

Saruni Eagle View is an exclusive safari lodge located in the heart of the Mara North Conservancy, bordering Kenya's renowned Masai Mara National Reserve. This eco-friendly lodge offers stunning panoramic views of the savannah plains and is renowned for its luxurious tented accommodations, each designed to blend seamlessly with the natural environment while providing modern comforts.

Guests can embark on guided game drives and bush walks led by expert Maasai guides, offering unique opportunities to encounter the region's abundant wildlife, including the Big Five. Saruni Eagle View also provides cultural experiences, such as visits to nearby Maasai villages, allowing guests to immerse themselves in the rich heritage of the local communities.

Entim Mara

Entim Mara is an intimate safari camp located in the heart of Kenya's Masai Mara National Reserve. Nestled among acacia trees along the Mara River, the camp offers a prime location for wildlife viewing, especially during the Great Migration.

Guests stay in spacious and comfortable tents, each with en-suite facilities and private verandas overlooking the savannah. Entim Mara specializes in providing authentic safari experiences, with expert guides leading game drives to encounter the Big Five and witness the spectacle of the Mara's wildlife.
